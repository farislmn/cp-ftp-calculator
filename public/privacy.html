Privacy Policy

AtUr Pace Planning and CP Calculator  ·  Effective date: 14 May 2026

AtUr Pace Planning and CP Calculator ("we", "our", or "the app") is committed to protecting your privacy. We will never access, use, or share your data without your explicit consent.

Who we are

AtUr Pace Planning and CP Calculator is a web application for runners that calculates Critical Power (CP) and W′ from training data and generates race pacing prescriptions. The data controller is AtUr Pace Planning and CP Calculator. For privacy enquiries, contact us at faris.slmn@gmail.com.

What data we collect

  • Account information: your email address, used to create and identify your account.
  • Intervals.icu credentials (optional): your Athlete ID and API key, stored only when you choose to save them to your profile. These are used solely to fetch your training data from Intervals.icu on your behalf.
  • Progress Journal entries: Critical Power (watts), W′ (joules), R² fit quality, and the efforts used to calculate them. Stored only when you explicitly click "Save to Journal".
  • Technical data: anonymised usage metrics collected by our hosting provider (Netlify) for performance monitoring. We do not use third-party analytics trackers.

How we use your data

  • To authenticate you and associate your journal with your account.
  • To display your Progress Journal across sessions.
  • To fetch training data from Intervals.icu when you request it.

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with any third parties.

Third-party services

  • Supabase — our database and authentication provider. Data is stored on Supabase infrastructure. See supabase.com/privacy.
  • Google OAuth (optional): if you choose to sign in with Google, Google authenticates you and shares your email address with us. See Google's privacy policy.
  • Intervals.icu: if you connect your account, we access your activity data via the Intervals.icu API using your credentials. See intervals.icu/privacy.
  • Netlify: our hosting provider. See netlify.com/privacy.

Data retention and deletion

Your data is retained for as long as your account is active. You may request full account and data deletion at any time by emailing faris.slmn@gmail.com. We will process deletion requests within 30 days.

Your rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you.
  • Correct inaccurate data.
  • Request deletion of your account and all associated data.
  • Withdraw consent at any time (e.g. disconnect Intervals.icu access by removing your API key).

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at faris.slmn@gmail.com.

Age restriction

This service is intended for users aged 18 and over.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Significant changes will be communicated via email to registered users. The effective date at the top of this page will always reflect the latest version.
